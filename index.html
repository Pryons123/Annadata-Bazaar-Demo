Privacy and Refund Policy

Privacy Policy

We collect personal information including names, emails, and farm details to provide our services. Your data is kept secure and only used to facilitate transactions and communication between farmers and consumers. We do not sell your data to third parties. Cookies are used to enhance user experience. You may request data access or deletion by contacting support.

Refund Policy

All sales are final unless the product is significantly not as described. Consumers should contact the farmer directly to resolve any issues. Our platform facilitates connection but does not handle transactions directly.
